# Train Planning: Paris → Lyon → Annecy

## Paris → Lyon
- **Route:** Paris Gare de Lyon → Lyon Part-Dieu
- **Train:** TGV INOUI (~2h)
- [Book on SNCF Connect](https://www.sncf-connect.com/en-en/train/route/paris/lyon)

## Lyon → Annecy
- **Route:** Lyon Part-Dieu → Annecy
- **Train:** TER (~2h)
- [Book on SNCF Connect](https://www.sncf-connect.com/en-en/train/route/lyon/annecy)

## Notes
- Travel light, book early for cheaper tickets
