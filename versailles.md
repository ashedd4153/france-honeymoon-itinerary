## Overview & Logistics

### Why Stay?
- **The Experience:** You see the Hall of Mirrors empty at 9am while day-trippers are still on the train, and you end the day with a magical horse show that day-trippers can't easily stay for.
- **The Logistics:** Staying here positions you South of Paris. You catch the TGV from **Massy** to the Loire directly, skipping the chaotic transfer back to Montparnasse.

### Getting There & Away
**Distance from Paris:** ~12 miles (20 km).
- **From Paris (Uber):** ~30-45 mins. Easiest option with bags.
- **To Loire Valley:** Taxi/Uber to **Massy TGV** station (25 mins), then TGV directly to Saint-Pierre-des-Corps (1 hr).
