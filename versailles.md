## Overview & Logistics

### Rationale for Stay
- **Experience:** Access to Hall of Mirrors at opening (9:00 AM) and evening Equestrian Show.
- **Logistics:** Direct TGV connection from **Massy** to Loire Valley, bypassing Paris Montparnasse transfer.

### Getting There & Away
**Distance from Paris:** ~12 miles (20 km).
- **From Paris (Uber):** ~30-45 mins. Easiest option with bags.
- **To Loire Valley:** Taxi/Uber to **Massy TGV** station (25 mins), then TGV directly to Saint-Pierre-des-Corps (1 hr).
